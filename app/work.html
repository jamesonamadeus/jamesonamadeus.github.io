Problem

Decrease in the Retail Industry, Rise of the E-Commerce - Customers are becoming more conscious and educated shoppers, not only are they looking for the best deals but more importantly factoring in the time and effort that goes into the process. Due to growth of technology, the ease and access to online information is phenomenal, it is no surprise that the mega online retailer, Amazon, is able to increase their sales by 97% every year. It is true that the online shopping will never replace, 1:1, the experience of going into a brick-and-mortar store with a warmth greeting by a staff member, but in order for a business to thrive for the road head, they need to continually stay agile and competitive.

To rebrand Titleist and provide an unparalleled online experience that demonstrates the company’s values and mission, all while cultivating a community for golfers and sports enthusiasts alike.

Educause

A nonprofit association whose mission is "to advance higher education through the use of information technology"

Problem

Aligning technology’s greatest capabilities with Higher Education’s growing concerns - The advancement of higher education has made waves in development due to the growth of IT. Evidence is mounting that IT is a major differentiator and a integral key to success within the education field. Educause has outlined 10 topics that will need to be focused for the long-term betterment of higher education.

  1. Information Security: Developing a risk-based security strategy that keeps pace with security threats and challenges
  2. Student Success: Managing the system implementations and integrations that support multiple student success initiatives
  3. Institution-wide IT Strategy: Repositioning or reinforcing the role of IT leadership as an integral strategic partner of institutional leadership in achieving institutional missions
  4. Data-enabled Institutional Culture: Using BI and analytics to inform the broad conversation and answer big questions
  5. Student-centered Institution: Understanding and advancing technology's role in defining the student experience on campus (from applicants to alumni)
  6. Higher Education Affordability: Balancing and rightsizing IT priorities and budget to support IT-enabled institutional efficiencies and innovations in the context of institutional funding realities
  7. IT Staffing and Organizational Models: Ensuring adequate staffing capacity and staff retention in the face of retirements, new sourcing models, growing external competition, rising salaries, and the demands of technology initiatives on both IT and non-IT staff
  8. Data Management and Governance: Implementing effective institutional data governance practices
  9. Digital Integrations: Ensuring system interoperability, scalability, and extensibility, as well as data integrity, standards, and governance, across multiple applications and platforms
  10. Change Leadership: Helping institutional constituents (including the IT staff) adapt to the increasing pace of technology change

To read more about Educause's outline of the Top 10 Issues, click here.

Objective

To design and develop a search engine for the Educause Library, where the users have access to a database with over 21,000+ resources of research and publication, through a UX and accessibility lens.

Velir

A digital agency that designs and develops enterprise websites and digital marketing solutions - specializations include Sitecore, Adobe, Drupal, Data Visualization and Digital Strategy

Problem

Lack of Information Accessibility in the Digital Age - The use of internet has become a right, where every individual should have the same accessibility to the information on websites, applications, and online mediums. Many think that the “same” translates to having similar internet access, but in reality it is giving the same opportunity to access website content to individuals who have disability. Currently, government agencies, federal-funded nonprofits, public higher education institutions, and public K-12 schools are required to be Section 508 compliant. Many large companies are adopting better accessibility practices as well.

To read more about Section 508 Compliance, click here.

Objective

To implement design layouts into code that are Section 508 Compliant and web content accessible for all users.

Startframe

Rapid Boilerplate/Framework with a bit of Sass & Gulp to get your project started

Problem

Excessive Time to Upstart a Project - Too often do we see talented developers spend immense amount of time getting a simple project started or that they use too big of a tool for the task. There is no reason why a certain project should need to compile for several minutes just to get an app to say “Hello World”. Need more resources and support that the most important part of any project is to get it up running and out the door to the users.

Objective

To create an framework that is barebones and minimizes on the bloat and ready to go. Focusing on what is important - getting your project up and running live on the local server.
