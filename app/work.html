Wemote

Remote Job Matchmaker

Problem

Inaccessibility of Jobs - Automation is replacing conventional blue and white collar jobs by the the hundreds daily. Thus, positioning working families no choice but to move back into metropolitan area, to find job opportunities. Due to this migration, it is causing overcrowding, increase in gentrification, and most importantly, harming the surrounding economy of the urban areas.

Objective

To build a job matchmaking platform that aims to provide more accessible remote opportunities to job seekers in the non-metropolitan areas.
