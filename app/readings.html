readings

a collection of books that I have enjoyed and would recommend to any startup enthusiast

  • Delivering Happiness: A Path to Profits, Passion, and Purpose by Tony Hsieh
  • Shoe Dog: A Memoir by the Creator of Nike by Phil Knight
  • Remote: Office Not Required by Jason Fried & David Heinemeier Hansson
  • Zero to One: Notes on Startups, or How to Build the Future by Peter Thiel & Blake Masters
  • $100 Startup: Reinvent the Way You Make a Living, Do What You Love, and Create a New Future by Chris Guillebeau
  • Creativity, Inc.: Overcoming the Unseen Forces That Stand in the Way of True Inspiration by Ed Catmull & Amy Wallace
  • The Tipping Point: How Little Things Can Make a Big Difference by Malcolm Gladwell
  • Little Bets: How Breakthrough Ideas Emerge from Small Discoveries by Peter Sims
  • Black Box Thinking: Why Most People Never Learn from Their Mistakes--But Some Do by Matthew Syed
  • The Lean Startup: How Today's Entrepreneurs Use Continuous Innovation to Create Radically Successful Businesses by Eric Ries
  • The Start-up of You: Adapt to the Future, Invest in Yourself, and Transform Your Career by Reid Hoffman & Ben Casnocha
  • The 4-Hour Workweek by Timothy Ferriss
  • SuperBetter: The Power of Living Gamefully by Jane McGonigal
  • Naked Economics: Undressing the Dismal Science by Charles Wheelan
  • The 11 Laws of Likability: Relationship Networking . . . Because People Do Business with People They Like by Michelle Tillis Lederman
  • How to Have a Good Day: Harness the Power of Behavioral Science to Transform Your Working Life by Caroline Webb
  • Smartcuts: How Hackers, Innovators, and Icons Accelerate Success by Shane Snow
  • Rework by Jason Fried & David Heinemeier Hansson
  • The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy by Douglas Adams
